Sell by adding products to the cart

Follow the steps below:

  • Scan the product barcode (make sure the arrow in the computer is at the center of the screen) OR
  • For items without a barcode, type the product name/s on the search bar and add to the cart
  • To add more products, either click on the ‘+’ sign or click in the box and type the number of products purchased
  • To remove a product from the cart, click ‘x’ at the end of the product row or clear the entire cart by clicking the bin icon next to the sub total
  • Select your mode of payment at the RetailPay POS checkout
